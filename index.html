Hi.I'm Ricardo Nobangela.
A Multi-disciplinary creative.

My portfolio spans Illustration, Urban Planning, and Event Curation, blending artistic vision with practical spatial design. I don't just create; I translate the complex narrative of the city into tangible experiences, from the strokes on a physical canvas to the layout of a vibrant urban event.

My Inspiration is fueled by the dynamic energy and rich history of Cape Town. My work is a continuous endeavor to capture its spirit, documenting the community, culture, and character found in its streets, its people, and its unique geometry.
